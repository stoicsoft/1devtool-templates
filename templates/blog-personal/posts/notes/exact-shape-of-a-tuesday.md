A Tuesday I am happy with looks, from the outside, like most other Tuesdays. From the inside, it has a particular shape.

- 06:45 — wake up, read a page of something printed
- 07:30 — coffee, the first of two
- 08:00 — ninety minutes at the desk, one task
- 09:30 — walk, 35 minutes, no podcast
- 10:15 — second coffee, the short one
- 10:30 — another block, slightly harder task
- 12:30 — lunch, slow
- 13:30 — email, for the first time
- 14:30 — meetings if any, otherwise a third block
- 17:00 — done, regardless of what was done
- 17:30 — swim or run
- 19:30 — dinner, cooked
- 21:00 — reading, nothing urgent

Most Tuesdays don&rsquo;t look like this. The ones that do are the Tuesdays I end up writing about, a year later, as if they were the norm.
